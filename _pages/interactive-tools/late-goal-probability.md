# Late Goal Probability Dashboard

This dashboard is currently under development.

It will be a research prototype for estimating the probability of at least one more goal after a given match minute using in-play football market information.

Planned inputs include:

- Match minute
- Current score
- Current total goals
- Asian over/under line
- Over odds
- Under odds
- Asian handicap line
- Home and away handicap odds

Planned outputs include:

- Probability of at least one future goal
- Probability curve by minute
- Model probability versus market-implied probability
- Research-based value signal
