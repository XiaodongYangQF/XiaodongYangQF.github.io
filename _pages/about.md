## 👋 Hello, I’m Xiaodong!

I am a PhD candidate in Banking and Finance at UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School, University College Dublin.

My research focuses on option-implied information, tail risk, risk-neutral and physical distributions, density forecasting, and financial risk management.

I am particularly interested in how option markets reveal information about future market risks, equity premiums, variance premiums, and tail events.

I am also interested in quantitative finance, derivatives pricing, asset pricing, machine learning, and data-driven trading strategies.

## Selected Experience

### 🎓 Academic Background

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Banking and Finance at UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School. My research combines option-implied distributions, tail-risk modelling, density forecasting, and financial econometrics.

### 💼 Professional and Certification Background

I hold the CQF, FRM, and CMA certifications. I previously worked at Huawei Technologies as a financial settlement specialist and risk analyst.

### 📚 Teaching

I have teaching and tutoring experience in finance-related modules at University College Dublin, including financial economics, numerical methods, and derivative securities.


## Research Areas

### Option-Implied Information
Recovering risk-neutral distributions from option prices and studying their predictive information.

### Tail Risk and Extreme Events
Modelling downside and upside tail behaviour using GEV/GPD-based methods.

### Density Forecasting and Risk Management
Evaluating predictive densities, VaR, Expected Shortfall, and market risk forecasts.
